Privacy

Simple tracking for simple website limits.

Screen Time Manager only tracks the activity needed to provide website blocking, limits, schedules, and basic usage stats.

What the extension tracks

The extension tracks domain-level activity, time spent on configured sites, visit counts, blocked attempts, snoozes, and settings needed to enforce your limits.

What it is for

This data is used to block distracting websites, show daily usage stats, enforce scheduled focus blocks, and help you understand where your attention is going.

What it is not for

  • No selling user data.
  • No unnecessary tracking.
  • No broad surveillance of everything you do online.
  • No shame-based scoring or public sharing.

User control

You choose the sites, limits, and schedules. You can adjust or remove distracting domains from the extension settings when your focus rules need to change.
