FAQ

Questions before you install?

Short answers for the things people usually want to know before adding a focus extension.

Is Screen Time Manager free?

The extension is free to install from the Chrome Web Store.

What can it block?

You can add distracting domains such as video, social, entertainment, news, or any site that tends to pull you off task.

Does it track everything I do online?

No. It is focused on domain-level activity, configured limits, blocked attempts, and usage stats needed for the product to work.

Can I change my rules later?

Yes. You control the domains, schedules, and limits, so your setup can change with your routine.
